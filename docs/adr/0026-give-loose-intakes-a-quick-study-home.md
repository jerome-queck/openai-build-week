# Give loose intakes a quick study home

Standalone questions, pasted mathematics, loose attachments, and similar Session Intakes will start immediately inside a built-in Quick Study area rather than requiring the learner to create or select organizational containers first. Quick Study supplies a system-owned Study Workspace and unfiled Study Mission so every Learning Session, Managed Asset, source relationship, Learning Trail, and piece of Understanding Evidence has durable ownership.

Quick Study is a normal resumable part of the local product, not a temporary or unsaved chat mode. Its sessions appear in history and can be consolidated, searched, exported, or resumed under the same rules as sessions in named workspaces.

The learner may later file a Quick Study session into a named Study Workspace and Study Mission. Filing preserves its Session Record, source and artifact relationships, Learning Trail, Understanding Evidence, verification provenance, and relevant Managed Assets rather than recreating the session or flattening it into an export.
