# Adopt Contributor Covenant 3.0

Clarifold will adopt Contributor Covenant 3.0 as its Code of Conduct rather than maintain a custom behavioral policy. The policy applies to the project's public community spaces even though Clarifold is source-available rather than open source. Conduct reports go privately to `conduct@jeromegroup.org`, which is controlled by sole owner and community manager Jerome Queck; public GitHub issues are not an acceptable channel for conduct reports. Receipt and replies from the dedicated address have been accepted as operationally verified. Clarifold may tailor the project name, reporting instructions, and responsible community manager, but must preserve the upstream attribution and clearly document any substantive adaptation. The conduct address must not double as the vulnerability-reporting channel.
