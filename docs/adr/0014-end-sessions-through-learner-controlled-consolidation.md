# End sessions through learner-controlled consolidation

Learning Sessions will not end automatically. The learner may invoke Finish & consolidate at any time, and the teaching system may suggest it when the Learning Goal appears sufficiently addressed, but the suggestion never prevents further work. Leaving the session without consolidation preserves its complete Session Record and keeps it available for resumption.

Leaving an unconsolidated session sets its Session Status to Paused. During Finish & consolidate, the learner assigns the Session Target a Target Disposition of Addressed, Deferred, or Unresolved. Addressed means that the learner considers the session's treatment sufficient for now; it does not assert mastery. Deferred and Unresolved preserve the distinction between intentionally postponing work and reaching the current limit without settling it.

Session Consolidation will present a concise review of the Trail Draft, demonstrated Learning Progress, unresolved questions, and proposed next step before creating a Consolidated Session Outcome. The learner may revise what is retained and which Learning Artifacts are included rather than accepting an agent-generated final package blindly.

Session Status and understanding will remain separate. Consolidated means that the learner chose to package the session's current outcome; it does not mean that every concept was understood or mastered. A Consolidated Session Outcome may explicitly show that material was only explored, an Understanding Check was skipped, or a Verification Gap remains. Stronger learning claims require corresponding Understanding Evidence.

A consolidated Learning Session remains a stable historical record. Choosing `Continue this work` creates a linked Continuation Session under the same Study Mission rather than reopening and appending new teaching activity to the old Session Record. Deferred and Unresolved outcomes present continuation prominently as their next action, while an Addressed outcome may still be continued voluntarily. Relevant goals, Return Points, unresolved questions, artifacts, and Understanding Evidence remain linked and available as context without duplicating the earlier record.

Consolidation does not freeze Learning Artifacts. They remain directly editable through Artifact Revisions and Section Regeneration under their existing provenance rules; artifact editing alone does not reopen the teaching session.

The consolidated Learning Trail will use progressive disclosure. Its Trail Overview contains the Learning Goal, central insight, essential reasoning path, demonstrated evidence, unresolved points, and next step. Proof walkthroughs, Concept Peeks, Teaching Variants, verification evidence, source links, and included notes remain available as expandable detail instead of lengthening the first view. A Required Trail Item remains visible through consolidation and regeneration until the learner removes the requirement.
